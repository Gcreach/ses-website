More about us

We are Storm Eye Studios, an independent video game studio located in Phoenix, Arizona. We started as a group of friends with different talents who came together to make games. Since then, we've expanded into 10+ members working on a major Unreal Engine project.

SES has made efforts to connect with gamers starting with back to back EVO trips in 2024 and 2025. We also hosted a launch party for our first game, Blade of the Yokai.
